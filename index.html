Calculation Rules & Formulas

Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA): Calculated as the weighted sum of credit points earned in a specific semester divided by total credits attempted in that semester.

SGPA = Σ (Course Credits × Grade Point) ÷ Σ (Course Credits)

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA): Calculated as the arithmetic average of SGPAs of all completed semesters (semesters with registered subjects and non-zero credits).

CGPA = (SGPA₁ + SGPA₂ + ... + SGPAₙ) ÷ n   [for all completed semesters n]
